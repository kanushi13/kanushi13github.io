Adolescents for Animals

What is Adolescents for Animals?

Adolescents for Animals is a non-profit organization that looks to promote animal welfare while getting teenagers in the community involved. Through fundraisers and other public events, we work to reach, educate, and encourage as many students as possible in this humanitatiran cause while supporting other local animal welfare organizations.

About Us

Our Mission is to promote animal welfare and encourage adolescents to join this humanitarian cause and give them an opportunity to be part of their community.

From a young age, Victoria Li has always gravitated towards animals. She loves to interact with animals she comes across, including her own dog, while dreaming about being a veterinarian. This undying love carries over to her passion for animal welfare, a cause that represents the values of compassion, responsibility, and respect. Subsequently, in December of 2022, Adolescents for Animals was created! Adolescents for Animals is a non-profit organization that looks to promote animal wellbeing and ecourages adolescents to join this humane cause, all the while giving them an opportunity to be part of their community. Shortly after, she recruited two individuals; Kali Bucklen and Shahzad Bolduc, both equally passionate about this cause. Through fundraisers and other public events, this organization works to reach, educate, and encourage as many people (specifically students) as much as possible in this humanitarian cause. The goal is to support other animal welfare organizations, and benefit as many animals as we can by providing them safety and comfort. Although currently we work in DelCo community, we hope to slowly spread our organization to the whole state of Pennsylvania, and in the future, to the United States as a whole.
